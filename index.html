Introduction

Pokémon Legends: Z-A takes place in Lumiose City, which is currently undergoing an urban renewal project aimed at creating a space shared by both humans and Pokémon. Upon arriving in Lumiose, players will meet and bond with their first partner Pokémon—choosing from Chikorita, Tepig, or Totodile—and start their adventure as a Pokémon Trainer.

Pokemon Starter
